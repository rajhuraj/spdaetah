About Us

Mr. Raghuraj Singh

Soldier Physical Defence Academy (SPDA) Etah, Uttar Pradesh mein sthit hai. Yahan students ko Army, Police, BSF, CRPF jaise government exams ke liye physical ki tayari karwayi jaati hai.

Raghuraj Sir Indian Army mein 24+ saal seva ke baad ab students ki jindagi badal rahe hain. Unka ek hi lakshya hai — har student ka selection ho.

  • Army / Navy / Air Force physical training
  • Police & constable physical preparation
  • Running, long jump, chest, height training
  • Personal guidance aur regular practice
  • Hostel mein WiFi — online classes ke liye
